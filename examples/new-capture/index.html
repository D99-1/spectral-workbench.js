Spectral WorkBench



Create a spectrograph. You should have three images:

  1. The sample image (the thing you are analysing). It could be something like a gas discharge emission spectrum.
  2. Calibration image. It is a spectrum taken using your spectrograph of something that you know the emission spectrum of, with clear visible peaks. It could be the emission spectrum of a CFL bulb.
  3. Full spectrum image. It is an image taken of a full spectrum. This is used to identify the start and end points of your spectrograph.

Ensure that all spectrums are taken from the same distance from the light source or calibration may be inaccurate.
